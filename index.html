Installation Guide

  • First of all download the zip file on your device ( repository.denver-2.0.2.zip ).
  • Go to the Kodi Settings.
  • Then click on Add-ons.
  • Click on "Install from zip file"
  • Go-to The path for the zip file location.
  • & Select the "repository.denver-2.0.2.zip"
  • Now you got the pop-up message on your screen top right corner.( Denver Repository Added )
  • Go-to the "Install from repository".
  • Select the "Denver Repository"
  • Go into the "Video add-ons" section & select the JioTV+ repo, and install the Jio TV.

    • Login method

  • Go-to the home screen.
  • Go-to Add-ons & long press the JioTV logo.
  • Select the settings, Go-to Account.
  • Click login & login with your Jio number & OTP or number & password.
  • if you forget your password then reset your Jio number password.
